### Introduction
The DirectX Diagnostic Tool (DxDiag) gathers comprehensive information about your graphics, display driver modules, audio devices, and system memory allocations to pinpoint card crashes.

### Symptoms & Root Causes
- Discovered video stuttering, DirectX system crashes, or lack of audio interface linkages.

### Deep Resolution Methods
To open the utility and examine your local device information:

1. Press `Win + R` keys on your keyboard.
2. Type `dxdiag` inside the prompt and press Enter.
3. Review the "Display" and "Sound" tabs for error lists.
